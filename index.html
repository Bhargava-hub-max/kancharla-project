OUR STAFF

kodali Sri Venkateswara Rao(NANI)

kodali Sri Venkateswara Rao(nani) is an Indian politician. He is a former member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly. He represented the Gudivada assembly constituency from 2004 to 2024 in the Andhra Pradesh Legislative assembly

Roja Selvamani

Roja, also known as Roja Selvamani, is an Indian actress and politician from Andhra Pradesh. She has acted in over 100 films in Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, and Malayalam. She currently serves as the Minister for Tourism, Culture, and Youth Advancement in the Andhra Pradesh government.

Gudivada Amarnath

Gudivada Amarnath is an Indian politician from the state of Andhra Pradesh. He is elected as the Member of the Legislative Assembly from Anakapalle Assembly constituency in 2019 to the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly representing the YSR Congress Party.
