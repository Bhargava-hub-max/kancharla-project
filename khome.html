ABOUT US

Kancharla Foundation is committed to creating a better future for underprivileged communities.

Founder:
Dr. Srikanth C Kancharla, a B.Tech, MBA, and MIE professional.
Mission:
To eradicate poverty and empower rural communities through various programs.
Key Areas of Focus:
Education: Empowering the next generation through learning initiatives.
Healthcare: Providing essential medical support and promoting health awareness.
Sustainable Solutions: Promoting organic farming and other long-term development projects.
Community Upliftment: Addressing the root causes of poverty and fostering self-sufficiency.
